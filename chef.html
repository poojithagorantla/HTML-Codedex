Omlete Receipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Green Chillies
  • Salt
  • Pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Break eggs in a bowl
  2. Add chopped onions,green chilles, salt and papper in the bowl and wisk it
  3. Add some oil in a pan and heat it up
  4. Pour our egg in the pan and let it fry on both sides
  5. Omlete is ready to eat
